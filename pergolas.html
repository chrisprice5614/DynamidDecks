Pergolas & Outdoor Living

Extend Your Home Outdoors

At Dynamic Decks, it's our goal to make your outdoors a place you'd like to be. Not only do we design and build high quality, long lasting decks, but we also build top of the line covers for any outdoor living needs. Our goal is to create a haven of relaxation outside, using premium materials and expert craftsmanship, our pergolas serve as a focal point that enhances any landscape.

Operable Pergolas

Elevate your outdoor experience with these innovative operable pergolas, designed to seamlessly blend functionality with style. These represent high quality comfort and luxury, offering versatile solutions to enhance the beauty and usability of your backyard. Whether you seek a shaded retreat from the sun, or an open-air space for entertaining, these state-of-the-art pergolas allow for unmatched flexibility and style.

Contemporary Pergolas

Contemporary pergolas are stylish, durable, and customizable! Built with high quality materials, these pergolas are built to withstand the elements, ensuring long-lasting beauty and minimal maintenance. This spin on the classic pergola features clean, straight lines, offering a minimalist aesthetic that complements various architectural styles. This sleek, modern look is perfect for homeowners seeking a stylish yet practical outdoor edition.

Each pergola is to your specification, utilizing high end CAD systems for precision manufacturing, we aim for a high attention to detail, ensuring that every pergola not only meets, but exceeds industry standards, providing exceptional structural integrity and aesthetic appeal. These contemporary pergolas are an excelent value with not only their looks, but also their durability. The low maintenance requirements mean that you can enjoy your pergola without the ongoing costs and efforts associated with other outdoor structures.

Classic Pergolas

These stylish pergolas, with decorative corbels that extend from the edges, bring a classic look with modern strength, making them a great addition to any outdoor space. Made from high-quality aluminum, they are built to last through all kinds of weather while keeping their beauty. The lattice roof provides partial shade, creating a nice mix of sun and shade.

Motorized Control

Operating your pergola is effortless thanks to the "slow motion" motorized actuators, capable of handling up to 500 lbs of push/pull force. An integrated electrical raceway discreetly conceals the power cable, ensuring a sleek and seamless appearence in your outdoor oasis.

Skyview Pavillions

We believe your patio should feel like a natural extension of your home—a welcoming space where you can unwind, entertain guests, and take in the beauty of the outdoors. That's why Skyview Pavilion patio covers are designed to bring your vision of a covered outdoor retreat to life. These innovative structures feature durable polycarbonate roofs that let in natural sunlight while shielding you from harsh UV rays and unpredictable weather. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering or simply enjoying a quiet moment outside, these pavilions provide the perfect balance of light and protection. With Skyview Pavilions, you can embrace the outdoors without worrying about sunburns, sudden rain showers, or excessive heat, making your patio a comfortable space to enjoy year-round.
